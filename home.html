Experience the perfect blend at our café

Welcome to La café masion, where every sip of coffee and every bite of our delicious food is made with love and passion. Our goal is to provide our customers with a warm and cozy comfortable environment where you can relax, catch up with friends, come on a first date or get some work done while relishing our food and drinks.


Whether you're in the mood for a caffe Mocha, a vanilla cream cold brew or a delicious slice of a cake, we’ve got you covered. We get most of our ingredients from our local farmers to help support our community.


Come visit us and experience the excellent hospitality and the magic of our café for yourself. Our friendly staff is always ready to welcome you with a smile and help accommodate your dietary restriction if needed. Hope to see you soon at La café masion.
